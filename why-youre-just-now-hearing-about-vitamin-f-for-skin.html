Why You're Just Now Hearing About Vitamin F for Skin

The latest launch from Drunk Elephant is a hydrating overnight mask featuring vitamin F — but what exactly is this mysterious up-and-coming ingredient?

Drunk Elephant

With the launch of Drunk Elephant's F-Balm Electrolyte Waterfacial — a hydrating overnight mask featuring vitamin F — we started wondering why we haven't heard about this seemingly potent vitamin before now. Well, it turns out that Vitamin F is not actually a vitamin at all, nor is it new. Vitamin F is a term that refers to two essential fatty acids (the "F" in vitamin F stands for fat) — linolenic acid (omega-3) and linoleic acid (omega-6). These acids are necessary for the growth and function of healthy cells, but the body can't create them on its own so they must be provided via food or topically.
